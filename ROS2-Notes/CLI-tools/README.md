# What is CLI?
**CLI** stands for **Command-Line Interface**.
It is a way to talk to your computer by typing words instead of clicking icons.

- **Normal computer (GUI)**: You click on pictures (icons) to open things.  
- **Command Line (CLI)**: You type commands like "open folder" or "show me my files," and the computer does it.  

Imagine it like this: Instead of pointing at a menu with your finger, you tell a waiter exactly what you want with your voice.
