# Greedy Algorithm

A **greedy algorithm** is an approach to problem-solving that makes the **best choice at each small step** with the hope that these local optimum choices will lead to a global optimum solution.

## Key Characteristics

- **Makes locally optimal choices** - picks what seems best at the current moment
- **Never reverses decisions** - once a choice is made, it's final
- **Simple and intuitive** - often easier to implement than other approaches
- **No backtracking** - doesn't reconsider previous decisions

## How It Works

1. **Start** with an empty solution
2. At each step, **make the choice** that looks best right now
3. **Move** to the next subproblem
4. **Repeat** until solution is complete
